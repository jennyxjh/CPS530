Things You Didn't Know About Israel

Fact #1

Israel is One of the World's Safest Countries.

Despite its geopolitical challenges, Israel is considered one of the safest countries for tourists, with a low crime rate and a strong focus on public safety.

Fact #2

Israel's Compulsory Military Service.

In Israel, military service is mandatory for most citizens at the age of 18. This practice has shaped the country's culture and societal structure, fostering a sense of community and responsibility.

Fact #3

Israel's known as the "Start-Up Nation".

Due to its high number of start-ups and tech companies per capita. The country invests heavily in research and development, leading to innovations in various fields such as cybersecurity, agriculture, and medicine.
